For integer represented using 2's complement the leftmost bit weight is
-2^31, for an integer to be negative it need to have the leftmost bit
set to 1.

smallest negative (-2^31)	largest negative (-1)
(10...0)			(1...1)

smallest positive (1)		largest positive (2^31 - 1)
(0...01)			(01...1)

zero (0)
(0...0)
