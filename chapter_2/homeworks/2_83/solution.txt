A. n = Y / (2^k - 1)

B. (a) y = [101], k = 3, Y = 5

       n = 5 / (2^3 - 1)
	 = 5 / (8 - 1)
	 = 5/7

   (b) y = [0110], k = 4, Y = 6

       n = 6 / (2^4 - 1)
         = 6 / (16 - 1)
         = 6 / 15
         = 2/5

   (c) y = [010011] k = 6 Y = 19

       n = 19 / (2^6 - 1)
	 = 19 / (64 - 1)
	 = 19/63
